Help Center

  • To use the chat, simply click on the "Speak" button and say your command or question.
  • You can also continue the conversation by saying the command "Continue."
  • To view your chat history, click on the "History" button in the top left corner of the screen.
  • To access the provided links, click on the "Links" button in the top right corner of the screen.
  • To go offline, say the command "Go Offline." This will return you to the landing page.
  • To clear the chat, say the command "Clear Chat." This will remove all of the messages from the chat history.
